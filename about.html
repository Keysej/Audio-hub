About SoundDrop

🎵 What is SoundDrop?

SoundDrop is a collaborative platform for ephemeral sound research and exploration. It brings together researchers, artists, and curious minds to share, discuss, and reflect on the sounds that shape our daily experiences.

🌟 How it Works

  • Daily Themes: Each day features a new audio exploration theme
  • Record & Share: Capture sounds directly from your device or upload audio files
  • Ephemeral Nature: All content disappears after 24 hours, encouraging authentic, in-the-moment sharing
  • Collaborative Discussions: Comment and discuss each other's audio contributions
  • Cross-Device: Works seamlessly across phones, tablets, and computers

🔬 Research Focus

This platform explores how we relate to sound in our daily lives - from urban soundscapes to emotional triggers, from memory-inducing audio to the sounds of productivity. By sharing these sonic moments, we build a collective understanding of our auditory world.

🤝 Community Guidelines

SoundDrop is a space for respectful exploration and genuine curiosity. Share sounds that contribute to our collective understanding, engage thoughtfully with others' contributions, and embrace the ephemeral nature of the platform.

📱 Get Started

Simply visit the main page, check today's theme, and start sharing! No account needed - just your curiosity and willingness to explore the sonic world around you.
